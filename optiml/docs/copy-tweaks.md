# Optiml — Copy Tweaks

**Purpose:** recommended copy swaps for the Fund Manager role page  
**Goal:** keep the stronger fund-manager positioning while bringing the messaging closer to the current product truth on the live site

---

## 1. Hero

### Current

**H1:** Your IC wants a Decision Record. Not another spreadsheet.

**Subhead:** Optiml produces structured, auditable outputs that hold up in Investment Committee reviews, lender conversations, and LP oversight. When constraints change — rates, valuations, regulatory thresholds — the record refreshes. You don't rebuild from scratch.

### Why change it

- Keeps the stronger committee-oriented framing
- Softens the leap from current product language into the newer "Decision Record" idea
- Better reflects the current site's emphasis on analysis, optimisation, and portfolio strategy

### Recommended replacement

**Step 2 — Optimisation runs**  
The engine works across the KPIs your IC tracks: ROI, Capex, NOI, CO₂ reduction, EPC ratings, and CRREM compliance. Optiml evaluates trade-offs across the same input base, so teams can align around one logic set instead of conflicting assumptions.

**Step 3 — Decision output out**  
A structured output with visible assumptions, traceable logic, and a refresh path when constraints change. Download as PDF or Excel. Use it directly in committee, lender, and internal review conversations.

**Note:** AI supports data enrichment and completion. The optimisation and recommendation logic remain structured, transparent, and reviewable.

### Why change it

- Removes the strongest overclaim: "The same inputs produce the same outputs, every time"
- "Decision output out" is awkward as a step label, but safer than asserting a fully established product category
- Avoids the stark "algorithmic, not generative" phrasing unless product wants to make that distinction explicitly

### Alternate step label option

If you want to keep the newer category language, use this instead:

Then keep the revised body copy beneath it.

## 7. FAQ

### Current

**Q: How is this different from the BI or ESG tools we already use?**  
Those tools produce data. Optiml produces a Decision Record — a structured output with a traceable logic chain, built to hold up in IC and lender review. It's not a reporting layer. It replaces the process of producing committee-ready decisions.

**Q: Can the Decision Record be audited independently?**  
Yes. Every output includes input assumptions, optimisation logic, and KPI outputs in a format auditors and lenders can follow. Nothing is opaque in the engine.

### Recommended replacement

**Q: How is this different from the BI or ESG tools we already use?**  
Those tools help you monitor, report, and analyze performance. Optiml helps you turn that analysis into a structured recommendation with visible assumptions, trade-offs, and outputs that can be used in IC, lender, and internal review conversations. It is not just another reporting layer.

**Q: Can the output be reviewed or audited independently?**  
Yes. Optiml surfaces the assumptions, KPI logic, and recommendation structure behind each output so teams, lenders, and external reviewers can follow how a conclusion was reached.

### Why change it

- "It replaces the process" is a bigger claim than the current site supports
- "Nothing is opaque in the engine" is too absolute
- "Reviewed or audited independently" is safer than promising full auditability if that term has legal or technical implications

## 9. Recommended Positioning Rule

If you want the page to stay commercially sharp without drifting away from the current product truth, use this rule across all rewrites:

**Optiml is still the optimisation / strategy / decision-intelligence system. "Decision Record" or "decision output" is the commercial expression of what that system produces for Fund Managers.**

That keeps the page differentiated without making it sound like a completely different product from the one currently described on the live site.
